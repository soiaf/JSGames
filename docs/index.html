9 Letter Word Find the 9 letter word hidden in the grid.
Four In A Row Click on a column to drop a disc. Try to get 4 red discs in a row, either horizontally, vertically or diagonally.
Hangman Try to guess the hidden word to save the life of the prisoner.
Minesweeper There are 14 mines hidden on the board. Clearing a square will show how many mines are adjacent to the square. You must mark all the mines and clear all the other squares to win.
Reflexes Try to catch 25 rabbits in the shortest time.
Reversi Also known as Othello, the goal of the game is to have the majority of disks be black by the end of the game.
Rock Paper Scissors A simple game where you choose from rock, paper or scissors. Rock beats Scissors, Paper beats Rock, Scissors beats Paper.
Sliding Squares Shuffle the board, then try to get it back in the correct order. Clicking on a square beside the hole moves that piece into the hole.
Tic Tac Toe You play X - try to get 3 in a row either horizontally, vertically or diagonally.
